Incinerator Usage Guide and Safety Tips

How to Use.

  • Open the loading door and place used pads inside.
  • Pour a small amount of paraffin on the pads to aid combustion.
  • Carefully light the pads, ensuring the flame takes hold.
  • Close the door immediately to begin the incineration process.
  • After incineration is complete, remove and empty the ash cup as needed.

Safety Tips!

  • Ensure the door is fully closed after lighting to prevent heat exposure.
  • Allow the unit to cool before emptying the ash cup.
  • Use paraffin sparingly to avoid excess flames.
  • Regularly clean and maintain the incinerator to ensure optimal performance.
  • Keep flammable materials away from the incinerator.
